FoodieFun Purpose

As an avid foodie, you love going out to eat. Wouldn't it be great to have a way to remember what you've ordered at each place, what you liked and didn’t like, what the wait time was a certain times of day, or what the service was like?

FoodieFun Features

  • Review food and drinks from any business
  • Easily create and post reviews of menu items
  • Easily edit or delete reviews
  • Add photos to your reviews
  • Share your reviews with friends
